# Fisc

Smart Investment Tax Planning platform for Indian investors.

## About

Fisc is a comprehensive tax optimization platform designed specifically for Indian investors to analyze, optimize, and manage their investment portfolio taxes efficiently.

## Features

- **Portfolio Tax Analyzer** - Analyze STCG/LTCG implications and optimize investment strategy
- **Regime Optimizer** - Compare old vs new tax regimes and choose the optimal one
- **Scenario Simulator** - Model what-if scenarios and their tax implications
- **Tax-Saving Insights** - Get personalized recommendations to minimize tax liability
- **Education Hub** - Learn tax concepts with guides, calculators, and glossary
- **Tax Calendar** - Track important deadlines and compliance requirements
- **FiscAI Assistant** - AI-powered tax chat support for instant answers
- **Holding Period Optimizer** - Optimize timing for better tax efficiency
